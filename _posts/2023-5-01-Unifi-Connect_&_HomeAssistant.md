# Tl;dr

Unifi Connect is a new product line that aims at providing a digital signage solution without subscriptions or dependencies on the cloud should your needs dictate staying off cloud solutions. Currently, the Connect application can be run on Unifi Cloud Key Gen 2. See the [Unifi Connect](https://www.ui.com/unifi/unifi-connect/) page for more information. 
You can also see my [Unifi Connect First Impressions](https://zavalalabs.github.io/2023/4/24/Unifi-Connect-First-Impressions.html) post for more information.

# My Use case

Often when I start working on a project such as this I find myself looking to mimic enterprise solutions in the home at a cost that won't break the bank. (Granted this 'break the bank' is subject to your wallet.) Not to mention I aim to try and provide solutions in my implementations to meet some needs of people who have a variety of disabilities.
